Common questions

Solar FAQs —
Answered Simply.

How long does installation take?
Most residential systems (1–5kVA) are installed within 1–2 days. Larger commercial systems may take 3–5 days depending on complexity and roof size.
What happens on cloudy or rainy days?
Solar panels still generate power in cloudy conditions — just at reduced output (20–50%). Your battery bank stores enough energy to cover periods of low generation.
How long do the batteries last?
Lithium batteries last 10–15 years with minimal maintenance. Tubular gel batteries last 5–7 years. We recommend lithium for long-term value.
Do you offer maintenance after installation?
Yes. We offer periodic maintenance checks, panel cleaning, battery health tests, and repairs. We also remain available via WhatsApp for any technical questions.
Can I expand my system later?
Absolutely. Our systems are designed to be expandable. You can add more panels, batteries, or upgrade your inverter as your power needs grow.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we offer flexible payment arrangements. Contact us to discuss what works for your budget — we want to make solar accessible to every home and business.
