Common questions

Before You Contact Us —
Quick Answers.

Is the site visit and quote really free?
Yes, completely. We visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a written quote with zero charge and zero obligation to proceed.
How quickly can you start a project?
For urgent jobs, we can begin within 24–48 hours of agreement. Larger projects are scheduled based on our team availability — we'll confirm your start date in writing.
Do you work outside Abuja?
We primarily serve Abuja FCT, but for large commercial or solar projects we travel to neighbouring states. Contact us to discuss your location and we'll advise if we can help.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. We understand solar and large wiring projects can be significant investments. We offer flexible payment arrangements — contact us to discuss what works for your budget.
Can I get quotes for multiple services at once?
Absolutely. Many clients combine solar installation with wiring upgrades or CCTV. We can assess and quote for all services in a single site visit, often saving you money overall.
What information should I have ready when I contact you?
Knowing your property type, location, and roughly what you need (e.g. solar for a 3-bed house, or rewiring an office) is enough to get started. Our engineer will gather the rest during the site visit.
