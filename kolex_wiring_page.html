Why it matters

Bad Wiring is a Danger You Can't See.

Most electrical fires, electrocutions, and equipment damage in Nigerian homes and offices trace back to one root cause — substandard or poorly installed wiring. The problem is invisible until it becomes a crisis.

Whether you're building new, renovating, or living in an older property, getting your wiring assessed and done right is one of the most important safety decisions you can make.

Electrical firesOverloaded circuits and undersized cables are the leading cause of house fires in Nigeria.
Electrocution riskExposed wires, ungrounded installations and missing RCDs put your family at serious risk daily.
Appliance damageVoltage fluctuations from poor wiring destroy refrigerators, TVs, ACs and other expensive equipment.

How we work

Our Wiring Process.
No Surprises.

1

Site visit & assessment

Our engineer visits your property to understand the layout, power requirements, existing installation (if any), and the scope of work needed. This is free of charge.

2

Wiring plan & material list

We prepare a full wiring plan — socket positions, circuit layout, DB location, cable routing — and a detailed material list with quantities and specifications.

3

Clear quotation & agreement

You receive a written quote covering labour and materials. No hidden costs. We agree on timeline and start date before a single cable is touched.

4

Professional installation

Our certified electricians carry out the wiring work neatly and efficiently. Conduits are used throughout, cable routing is clean, and we keep the site tidy at all times.

5

Testing & safety check

Every circuit is tested before handover — insulation resistance, continuity, earth loop impedance, and RCD trip times. We confirm everything is safe and working correctly.

6

Handover & documentation

We hand over a completed installation with your DB clearly labelled for every circuit. You receive a wiring certificate and we remain available for any questions after.

Common questions

Wiring FAQs —
Answered Simply.

How long does wiring a house take?
A standard 3-bedroom house typically takes 3–5 days. Larger or more complex properties may take longer. We'll give you a clear timeline in your quote.
Do I need to vacate the building during wiring?
For new builds, no. For occupied properties undergoing rewiring, we work room by room to minimise disruption. Power will be off to sections being worked on only.
What cables and materials do you use?
We use only standard-grade, certified cables (never substandard or counterfeit wires). All breakers, sockets, conduits and accessories are quality-brand components.
Can you wire for solar at the same time?
Yes — and we strongly recommend it. We can design and install your electrical wiring to be solar-ready from the start, saving you cost and disruption later.
Do you provide a certificate after the job?
Yes. Every completed job is issued a wiring completion certificate confirming the installation has been tested and is safe. Your DB will also be fully labelled.
Can you add more sockets or power points to my existing home?
Absolutely. Adding sockets, light fittings, outdoor points or dedicated circuits (for AC or appliances) is a common job we carry out quickly and neatly.
