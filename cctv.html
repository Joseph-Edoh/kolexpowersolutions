Common questions

CCTV FAQs —
Answered Simply.

How many cameras do I need for my home?
Most 3–4 bedroom homes are well covered by 4–8 cameras. We recommend covering the front gate, main entrance, back of the house and key indoor areas. Our engineer will advise during your free site visit.
Can I view my cameras on multiple phones?
Yes. Most NVR systems support multiple users. We can set up access for your spouse, family members or business partners on their own devices during installation.
How long is footage stored?
Standard systems loop-record over 30 days. We can configure longer storage (60+ days) with a larger hard drive, or add cloud storage so footage is backed up off-site.
Do the cameras work at night?
Yes. All our cameras include IR night vision with a minimum 20–30m range. We also offer colour night vision cameras that produce clearer images in low-light conditions.
What if my internet goes off?
Cameras continue recording to your NVR hard drive even without internet — you'll just lose remote viewing until the connection is restored. Local footage is never affected by internet outages.
Do you work on systems from other installers?
Yes. We can repair, upgrade or expand most existing CCTV systems regardless of who installed them — as long as it's a recognised brand like Hikvision, Dahua or similar.
